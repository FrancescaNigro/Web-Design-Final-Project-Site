The Leaning Tower of Pisa

Basic Facts about the Leaning Tower of Pisa:

Height: 186 feet
Floors: 7
Address: Piazza del Duomo, 56010 Pisa, Italy
Architectural style: Romanesque architecture
Function: Bell tower

The Leaning Tower of Pisa is a freestanding bell tower, and is known worldwide for its unintended tilt to one side.

Construction was done to this tower in order to ensure it would not completely fall down, mainly because of it is such a huge tourist attraction for Pisa. Although some work was done to straighten it out a bit, it is still quite obviously leaning!
